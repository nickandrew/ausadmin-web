aus.* Newsgroup Administration

Overview

Our purpose is to oversee the management of the aus.* newsgroups. We control the creation of new aus.* newsgroups and decide when old aus.* newsgroups should be deleted according to our current policy (see below).

Current operations are out of Zeta Internet with Nick Andrew looking after most of the administration work and me (Jason Nicholls) working on this web site.

Before sending any questions to Nick or me make sure you have read the relevant FAQs and are aware of our current policy. This web site should cover most, if not all, queries you may have. If something seems important to you and it's not available here then please send me some email.

Current Policy

  • Remove dead newsgroups
  • Promote a better structure whether that means moving existing 2nd level groups into 3rd level groups, or renaming others
  • Make sure ISPs respond to newgroup and rmgroup messages
